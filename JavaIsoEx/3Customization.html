How to Customize

This function makes you change your appearance by clicking the ">>" and "<<" sign.
There are the Body, Shirt, Headgear and Weapon that you can change.
(Note: You can use this function as long as you login as an Inhabitant.)
(Note2: This means that an Alien cannot change their appearance.)

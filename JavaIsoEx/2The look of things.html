The look of ICE World

Our ICE isometric World will have music adjusting slider, chatting and the minimap on the bottom of the world.

Chatting

There are 2 ways of chatting
  • Talking
  • Yelling

Talking

Talking will allow you to chat with other people in our ICE World.
Bubbles will pop on your character and show what you have said.
Talking is limited for 50 letters in a talk.

Yelling

Yelling is another way to chat with people in the ICE World.
Animation of your yell will pop up half of the screen.
Yelling is limited for 10 letters in a yell.

Walking

First step,you have to left click for choosing your character (you can choose just only yourself)
then your ICETIZEN charactor will have a small rectangle edge surrounding it.
Second step, right click for walking (selecting the position) and then your ICETIZEN will walk to that place which is selected
(Note: if your left click and drag, the small rectangle edge will surround all the characters within the drag,
but remind that you can only walk your own ICETIZEN)
